## Route Notes
- Norte is much less busy than other Camino routes
- **June 1 start is a good choice** — tail end of the sweet spot (May–early June)
- First 3 weeks (Basque Country → Asturias) will be quieter; final stretch into Galicia in late June starts to pick up
- Peak crowding is July–August, driven by both pilgrims and Spanish/European beach tourists hitting the northern coast — prices spike then too
- **Book Basque Country and Cantabria hotels soon** — San Sebastián, Bilbao, Santander, and Santillana del Mar fill up fast once coastal tourism ramps up in mid-June
- Recommended advance booking window: 3–6 months ahead for peak season
